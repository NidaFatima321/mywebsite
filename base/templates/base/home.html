ONE STORY-FROM THE START

SERVING THE NATION SINCE 1990.

How We Started? Where We Stand Today?

The foundation of this restaurant was laid by Ms. Naila Shaikh with an aim to facilitate the then daily workers with peacefull,clean environment and cheap food in Karachi. The workers absolutely loved the food and environment in cheaper amount and appreciated the restaurant very much which derived the owner to then expand the restaurant all over the country, Therefore many branches were opened all over Pakistan in different periods of time. All the restaurants were given equal respect and love which make us stand here today with almost 50 restaurants all over Pakistan.
We provide the facility of kitchen inspection to our guests 24/7 without any hesitation which we consider major driving force in our business.
